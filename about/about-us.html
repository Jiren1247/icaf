About Us
We are the national arts organization for American children and the global arts organization for the world’s children.
Since 1997, we have enabled and empowered them to reclaim and reshape the future.
We inspire children and kindle the “inner child” in professionals for collaborative innovation, conflict resolution, and futures thinking.
Theory of Change
The Arts Olympiad—our school art program—fosters students’ creativity so they can marshall the Artificial Intelligence Revolution by reimaging themselves as “Artist-Atheltes” with creative minds and healthy bodies, free to imagine and play.

The World Children’s Festival—a celebration of creativity, diversity, and unity at The National Mall across the U.S. Capitol—provides a transformative experience that develops mutual empathy for “a more perfect union” and motivates students to become “creative-empaths,” responsible for spearheading positive social change.

The ChildArt Magazine—our quarterly publication—broadens young readers’ horizons, fosters peer learning, and develops global competencies.

We conduct Healing Art Programs to revive faith in nature of child victims of natural disasters and Peace through Art Programs to restore trust in humanity of children in conflict zones.

To introduce the world to the imagination of its children, we organize child art exhibitions worldwide.

To give voice to children, we arrange youth panels at major conferences.

For children to honor their leaders, we have instituted the World Children’s Award.

To learn more, see ICAF’s publications, our impact, and history of achievements.
