Climate Change

Today, young people all over the world are at the forefront of environmental protection, concerned that this little blue planet is facing irreversible climate change.


The future belongs to the children, and they can shape it.

ICAF promotes young people’s hands-on work in creating a sustainable ecosystem. The World Children’s Festival opens with Health & Environment Day, where children host workshops for peer learning and collaboratively produce murals on the environment.

See ChildArt magazine on climate change.
ICAF has also partnered with the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, the Endangered Species Coalition, and the Association of Zoos and Aquariums, to promote the “Saving Endangered Species Youth Art Contest” and to select the winners.
